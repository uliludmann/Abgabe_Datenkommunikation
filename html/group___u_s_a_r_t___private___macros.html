◆ IS_USART_BAUDRATE

#define IS_USART_BAUDRATE (   __BAUDRATE__)    ((__BAUDRATE__) < 6000001U)

Check USART Baud rate.

Parameters
<strong>BAUDRATE</strong>Baudrate specified by the user. The maximum Baud Rate is derived from the maximum clock on F0 (i.e. 48 MHz) divided by the smallest oversampling used on the USART (i.e. 8)
Return values
Testresult (TRUE or FALSE).
